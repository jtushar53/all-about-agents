---
title: Role prompting
tier: intermediate
tags: [persona, role]
---

*"You are a..."* — a named role reshapes the output distribution. Concrete roles work best:

- "a senior copy editor at a newspaper" > "a writer"
- "a Rust reviewer who dislikes unsafe code" > "a programmer"

Layer in a mandate alongside: *"Always list 3 concrete improvements, then stop."*

Roles do not change the model's capabilities — they change the *style* and *priorities* it samples with.
