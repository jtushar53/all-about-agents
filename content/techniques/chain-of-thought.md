---
title: Chain of thought (CoT)
tier: intermediate
tags: [cot, reasoning]
---

"Let's think step by step." One line, measurable gains on reasoning benchmarks (Wei et al. 2022; Kojima et al. 2022).

Two ways to get it:

- **zero-shot**: add the phrase
- **few-shot**: every example shows the worked reasoning, not just the answer

With modern reasoning models, the provider runs CoT internally — you don't have to prompt for it, and asking for it can *hurt* quality.
